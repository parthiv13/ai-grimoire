# ai-grimoire

`ai-grimoire` **is built on a single core principle: the developer maintains the cognitive load.**

These skills are engineered for workflows where you remain the sole architect of the system. This isn't about offloading the thinking to an agent and hoping for the best; it is about keeping total control over every decision, path, and coding style, while using the AI strictly as an execution layer.

With these tools deployed:
- You Own the Architecture: You dictate the exact logical paths and engineering patterns the AI must follow.
- You Enforce the Style: You mandate the precise coding standards and paradigms it must strictly adhere to.
- The Agent Executes: The AI handles the mechanical heavy lifting entirely under your strict constraints, functioning as a precise extension of your own intent.

You do the reasoning. The grimoire ensures your tools execute it flawlessly.

## Make It Yours
Because these skills are meant to anchor your personal cognitive load, they shouldn't be static. Everyone codes and architectures differently.

Hack around with the logic, inject your own engineering dogmas, and adapt these files until they map perfectly to your development workflow. Make them your own. Enjoy.
